# SmileABC

# description

This is a funny app to help your child or the people who want to learn the letters of the alphabet.

Through the flash cards , we can learn the spelling of the alphabet. Tap the characters on the card,you can hear the sound and find something interesting of it.

The letter animation is a amazing magic,which can teach you the writings of the letter.

After familiar with the flash cards, there is a word puzzle game designed for you to learn the most common words about the letters. Following are the screenshots of the app.
