            # One Sentence

            One sentence per day, don't disrupt the rhythm

            # Link

            http://onesentenceone.herokuapp.com/

            # project overview

            # Origin

            I was a person who needed to pass the English test. I was fortunate to join an English learning WeChat group, where everyone helped each other. I learned a lot of things from this group, and finally got the ideal results.

            Looking back at the process of learning English, two major difficulties were encountered. One is that there is no systematic learning method, and the other is that there is no group that helps each other and keep practicing.

            For the first question, finally I found Peter Lai's "English Learning Methodology". For the second problem, I joined this English learning group. In this group, for a period of time, I insisted on practicing the "Daily Sentence" in the "Teacher Gu Public Account" every day. At the same time, other friends would also practice together.

            Sometimes, a good habit is often developed from a little perseverance every day. Coupled with partners who are struggling together, they will not feel hard to do it.

            So, I was thinking, can I use the "sentence every day" as an entry point to solve the two difficulties I encountered then?

            • "Daily Sentence", combining Peter Lai's methodology, practice the pronunciation, vocabulary, phrases and grammar of this sentence every day. And try to use your own words for an in-depth interpretation.
            • "One sentence a day", you can like, comment, and even play some small games with other people's translations, such as: translate pk, plant trees together. In this way, there is an opportunity to find a learning partner.
